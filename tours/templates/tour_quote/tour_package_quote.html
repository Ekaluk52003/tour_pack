🤖 AI Email Parser (Superuser Only)

📋 What AI Extracts:

  • Number of people - Required! ("I", "we", "2 people", "family of 4")
  • Destinations - Thailand cities only (Bangkok, Chiang Mai, Phuket, etc.)
  • Travel dates - Month or specific dates ("December", "Dec 15-28")
  • Duration - Trip length ("2 weeks", "10 days")
  • Hotels & Activities - Any mentions in the email

💡 Tip: Email should clearly state number of travelers for accurate pricing (1pax, 2pax, 4pax)

How the AI parser works (2 steps)

1. Extraction (Step 1) — reads the email and pulls out facts: customer name, number of people, which cities, dates/duration, and any hotel or activity mentions.

2. Matching (Step 2) — takes those facts and picks the actual hotels & services from your database for each city.

What is a standing instruction?

A rule you teach the AI to apply on every future parse — its long-term “memory”. It is appended to the prompt for the chosen step(s). Use Scope to control where it applies:

  • Both steps — applied to extraction and matching.
  • Extraction (Step 1) — only changes how facts are read from the email (e.g. “treat ‘honeymoon’ as 2 people”).
  • Matching (Step 2) — only changes hotel/service selection (e.g. “for couples prefer 4-star or above; always add an airport transfer”).
